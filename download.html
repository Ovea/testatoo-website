Download

In this download area, you will be able to download the distribution (binary and source).

Maven repository

Testatoo releases are downloadable from Maven Central.

If you wish to add Testatoo as a dependency in your projects, you can refer to the Testatoo JARs in the dependency section of your project build file descriptor:

                    <dependency>
                        <groupId>org.testatoo</groupId>
                        <artifactId>testatoo</artifactId>
                        <version>x.y.z</version>
                    <dependency>
